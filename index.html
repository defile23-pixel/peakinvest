FAQ
Common questions
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to your dashboard, click Withdraw Funds, enter your amount and wallet address. Withdrawal requests are reviewed and processed by our team, typically within 1–3 business days.
Is my investment protected?
All investments carry risk and the value of your portfolio can go down as well as up. You may get back less than you invest. We do not guarantee returns or capital protection.
Can I run multiple plans at once?
Yes — you can hold multiple active investments at the same time. Each investment is tracked separately in your dashboard.
What cryptocurrencies are accepted?
BTC, ETH, USDT (TRC20/ERC20), BNB, and LTC for both deposits and withdrawals.
When does my investment start?
Your investment becomes active once your deposit is confirmed on-chain, typically within 10–30 minutes of sending funds.
Are returns guaranteed?
No. Past performance is not a guarantee of future results. All investments involve risk including the possible loss of principal. Please invest only what you can afford to lose.
